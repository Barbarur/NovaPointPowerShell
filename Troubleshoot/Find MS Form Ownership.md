#Troubleshoot #Forms #Owner

<br>

1. Visit the **MS Form** you want to know its owner and _right-click_ on any empty area.

2. From the drop-down menu, select _Inspect_.

3. At the _Inspect_ panel, select _Network_ and enable _Preserve log_.

4. Fill-up the Form and submit it in order to collect the necessary logs.

5. On the Logs list there will be one named **_responses_**. You will see a URL where you will see the user ID right after _/users/â€¦_

6. Navigate to *Azure AD > Users list*.

7. At the search box paste the user ID you saw on the logs.
